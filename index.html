Planet J Real Life Action RPG

Planet J Macau is a Real Life Action Role-Playing Game Project that costs more than £20M and 8 years of time.

I worked there for years and had been taken part in different roles and aspects since the early stage of development.

In Planet J, the 90,000 sq. ft. indoor theme park is zoned into different villages / wild areas / palace / park, each player could experience a unique role-playing journey with a magic book (digital gaming device with NFC and location tracking),. There are numerous puzzle games (touch-screen puzzle solving), physical games (puzzle solving with interactive hardwares), interactive devices, collectable props, battle booths (monster battle with body gestures) and AR/VR game booths, etc. Players will come across both virtual and real-person NPCs, alongside to offer Quests and help to advance the story plot; players would need to solve puzzles, find items in the world and battle monsters. The progress will be saved continuously and players may come again next time to continue their journey.
